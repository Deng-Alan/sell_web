## ADDED Requirements
### Requirement: Public product showcase pages
The system SHALL provide public-facing pages that let visitors browse product information without logging in.

#### Scenario: Visitor lands on the homepage
- **WHEN** a visitor opens the website
- **THEN** the system shows the homepage with site introduction, featured products, category entry points, and contact entry points

#### Scenario: Visitor browses product list
- **WHEN** a visitor opens a category or search result page
- **THEN** the system shows products with image, title, short description, price, stock, and a contact action

#### Scenario: Visitor opens product detail
- **WHEN** a visitor selects a product
- **THEN** the system shows the product title, images, detail content, price, stock, and contact action
